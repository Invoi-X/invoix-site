Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between Desktop and Cloud Pro?
The Desktop plan is a one-time purchase that installs on your Windows PC. All your data is stored locally on your computer. Cloud Pro is a monthly subscription that lets you access your invoices from any device — your phone, tablet, laptop, or any browser. Your data is securely stored in the cloud and synced automatically.
Can I try before I buy?
Yes! The Free plan lets you create up to 5 invoices per month with all 6 templates. No credit card required. When you're ready for unlimited invoices and extra features, you can upgrade anytime.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. With the Desktop version, your data never leaves your computer. With Cloud Pro, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data, and you can export or delete everything at any time.
What currencies do you support?
Invoi.X supports USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, and INR. You can set a default currency or choose per invoice.
Do I get updates with the Desktop version?
Yes — the Desktop plan includes 1 year of free updates. After that, you can continue using the version you have forever, or renew updates for a discounted price. Cloud Pro always includes the latest version.
Can I switch from Desktop to Cloud Pro later?
Yes! You can export your data from the Desktop app and import it into Cloud Pro. We make it easy to move between plans.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not happy with your purchase, contact us within 14 days for a full refund — no questions asked.
